Soldering Club

WHO WE ARE

Soldering Club is a student organization at the University of Michigan dedicated to improving campus life for students. We work hard to acheive our goals and have a strong leadership team that has made a difference.

WHAT WE DO

We have many members who have made a difference on campus. It's easy to join and weekly meetings are Wednesdays at Noon on North Campus. Anyone is welcome to come and check out what we are about.

HOW TO JOIN

Please visit our Contact page and contact a board member to join our mailing list.
